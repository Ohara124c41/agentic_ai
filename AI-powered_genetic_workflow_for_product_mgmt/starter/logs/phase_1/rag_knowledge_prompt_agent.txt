What is the podcast that Clara hosts about?
Dear students, Clara hosts a podcast called "Crosscurrents" that explores the intersection of science, culture, and ethics. Each week, she interviews a diverse range of professionals, including researchers, engineers, artists, and activists, covering topics such as brain-computer interfaces, neuroplasticity, climate migration, LLM prompt engineering, decentralized identity, and indigenous knowledge systems. The podcast aims to delve into various cutting-edge and interdisciplinary subjects, providing a platform for discussions on the latest advancements and ethical considerations in these fields.
